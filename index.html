Jeremy and I have absolutely no hobbies or interests in common at all! I fact, we're pretty much complete opposites! But who needs stuff in common? It's over-rated!

More importantly, we have the same life goals, the same priorities, the same attitudes to people and money and work and the same sense of what's right and wrong in life. Our first Date

Diana showed up to their first date straight from the gym (with her hair still wet from the shower, as Jeremy never lets her forget). To her horror, the cozy bar she picked out for their first meeting was hosting an office happy hour, so it was absolutely packed and conversation was near impossible. Both still remember the first time they caught each other's eyes at the bar. After just a few moments, they quickly switched gears and headed to a nearby restaurant.

How he proposed

Jeremy proposed to her in November 2014 just as completed the NYC Marathon. A month later, Jeremy counter-proposed to Diana on the Brooklyn Bridge. They're now busy planning their Valentine's Day 2016 wedding and chasing after their dog, Cody.
